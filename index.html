Story



The Darkness Comes



  • The sake of your tribe lies solely in your hands. You alone must resist and slay the scourge of the endless night on your path to journey through Metnal's nightmares and defeat him.
  • Your focus, accuracy, and patience will be put to the ultimate test as you cleanse the world from his curse.
  • Stop the evil god's hordes and bring the light back while you delve into the mysterious past of the island of Az'kalar.

  • Only light must prevail, will you stand up to the challenge?

Gameplay



Bright Blast



  • The ultimate spell for crowd control. Upgrade and customize it as you see fit.
  • Stop your foes in their tracks and leave them vulnerable for a short duration.


Halo Burst



  • Your last hope to save you from tight situations. Upgrade this spell and add various effects to it as you progress.
  • Keep yourself alive with this proximity reclamation ability.


Bright Beam



  • Your basic spell, cast it by moving the joystick to slay foes around you.
  • Deal reliable damage to multiple enemies in a line.
